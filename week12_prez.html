Stock Price Over Time

Whats happening?

This is an examlpe of using vega layering to your advantage as a data visualist. A mark rule is used to aggregate the mean of the stock over time. This is price cap line disclosed for each stock. Essentially, this is a second chart layerd overlaying the main chart beneath. This allows us to make one data visualization from two independent aggregation sources. Ex: Google has the highest average stock price than its competetors by a significant margin.
